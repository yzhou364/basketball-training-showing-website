Meet Coach Tabb

From Southern Illinois to Division I.

Joshua Tabb was one of the top high-school basketball prospects in Southern Illinois, ranked among the best in the region for his 6-foot, two-way scoring edge. He attended Florida International before transferring to Tennessee, and went on to earn his degree at Lindenwood University.

Joshua competed at the Division I level at Tennessee and Northern Kentucky, where he developed the discipline, work ethic, and grit of a competitive guard — the same standard he brings to every athlete he trains today.

"JTElite was built to give athletes the structure, discipline, and opportunity needed to reach their full potential."

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What age groups do you work with? +

JTElite trains athletes from 8 to 18, with most one-on-one work focused on middle and high school players preparing for school, club, or AAU competition. Adult athletes are welcome on a case-by-case basis.

Where do sessions take place? +

Sessions run out of partner facilities in St. Charles, MO and the surrounding metro area. The specific gym is confirmed when you book based on availability and what your athlete is working on.

How are athletes evaluated before starting? +

Every athlete starts with a short intake conversation and a 60-minute baseline session. Coach Tabb uses that session to assess fundamentals, decision-making, and conditioning, then builds a development plan from there.

What is the cancellation and refund policy? +

Sessions can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours notice at no charge. Inside that window, the session is forfeited unless there is a documented illness or emergency.

Do you offer discounts for packages? +

Yes — multi-session packages, sibling rates, and team bookings are all available. Reach out with your situation and we'll put together a package that fits.
